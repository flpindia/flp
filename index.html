About FLP India

FLP India is a leading company offering work-from-home opportunities in telecalling. We focus on providing a flexible working environment with competitive salaries and benefits. Our mission is to empower individuals to work from the comfort of their homes and thrive in their careers.

FLP India Growth Roadmap

Step 1: Foundation

FLP India began as a small tele-calling platform with only 10 agents.

Step 2: Expansion

We adopted a work-from-home model with 500+ employees, creating job opportunities for thousands of people.

Step 3: Nationwide Reach

FLP India became a leading company in providing tele-calling services across the country.

Step 4: Digital Transformation

We implemented digital interfaces and automated systems to improve customer and employee experiences.

Step 5: Future Goals

Our goal is to create more than 100,000 work-from-home job opportunities by 2025. We offer permanent jobs to all our employees.
